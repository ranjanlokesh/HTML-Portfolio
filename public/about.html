About Me

Technology leader bridging business strategy and engineering delivery

Who I Am

I am a Technology Program Manager and Vice President at NatWest Group with over 20 years of experience in banking and financial technology. My career spans the full arc of software engineering — from hands-on mainframe development to leading enterprise-scale Agile Release Trains that coordinate delivery across mainframe, middleware, and digital platforms.

I lead Agile Release Trains and Core Banking technology programmes at NatWest Group, with 15 years of progressive leadership within the organisation. My expertise spans SAFe/Agile programme management, PI Planning, vendor governance, and cross-functional stakeholder alignment — bridging engineering delivery with business strategy to drive digital transformation, regulatory compliance, and platform modernisation with measurable outcomes.

My Journey

I began my career at Capgemini Consulting India, working on HSBC Card Services projects involving VisionPlus credit card software, fraud detection, and card-scheme migrations. From there, I moved through progressively senior roles at Satyam Computers (Royal Bank of Scotland), Attra InfoTech (GE Money Australia and HSBC), and ultimately NatWest Group — where I have spent the last 15 years.

At NatWest, I transitioned from a Software Designer and Subject Matter Expert on Core Banking mainframe applications to a Vice President leading Agile Release Trains. Today I drive end-to-end programme delivery using the SAFe Agile framework, overseeing PI Planning, vendor governance, and cross-functional stakeholder alignment for some of the bank's most critical technology initiatives.

What I Do Best

Programme Leadership

I lead Agile Release Trains and technology programmes from inception through delivery, ensuring alignment between business priorities and engineering roadmaps.

Banking Technology

Deep domain expertise in Core Banking, credit cards, payments, digital channels, and regulatory compliance across mainframe and modern platforms.

Stakeholder Alignment

I build consensus among senior business and technology leaders, manage vendor relationships, and navigate complex governance across global teams.

Continuous Learning

Recognition

Beyond Work
